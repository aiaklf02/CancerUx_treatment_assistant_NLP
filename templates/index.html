    About Us

    Your Cancer Treatement Assistant

    CancerUX is an intelligent medical assistant designed to support cancer patients and healthcare providers by delivering accurate, understandable answers to complex medical questions. Built with cutting-edge Natural Language Processing (NLP) and fine-tuned on domain-specific data using BioBERT, our chatbot offers personalized responses based on trusted cancer-related knowledge sources. Our mission is to enhance patient experience, reduce confusion during treatment, and assist clinicians in accessing key information quickly and efficiently all through a user-friendly conversational interface.

    • AI-powered medical Q&A trained on cancer-specific data using BioBERT
    • Personalized support for patients and assistance for healthcare providers

    Welcome to our AI-powered CancerUX assistant. Feel free to ask any Cancer-related questions or describe your symptoms.
